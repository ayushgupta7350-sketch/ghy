Frequently Asked Questions

📅 Do I need a reservation?

Walk-ins are welcome! For groups of 6+ or special events, we recommend calling ahead to ensure seating.

🚗 Is parking available?

Yes, we have free dedicated parking for both 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers right behind the cafe.

🌱 Do you offer vegan options?

Absolutely! We have oat milk, almond milk, and vegan pastries available daily.

🎉 Can I host an event at AG COFFEE?

Yes! Our space is available for birthdays, meetings, and small gatherings. Contact us for custom packages.

📦 Do you deliver outside Nagpur?

Currently we deliver within Nagpur city limits. We're working on expanding soon!

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, credit/debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and all major wallets.
