Our Story · Our Passion

More than just coffee — it's a culture, a craft, and a community that brings people together.

☕ The AG COFFEE Journey

Founded in 2020 by Ayush M. Gupta, AG COFFEE started with a simple dream — to serve the most authentic, high-quality coffee experience in Nagpur. What began as a small corner cafe has now become a beloved destination for coffee lovers, remote workers, and friends seeking warmth in every cup.

Our name "AG" represents both the founder's initials and the spirit of "Authentic Goodness". We believe that coffee is not just a beverage, it's an emotion. That's why we source our beans directly from sustainable farms in Coorg and Chikmagalur, roasting them in small batches to preserve the rich, complex flavors.

Every cup at AG COFFEE tells a story of dedication — from the farmer who nurtured the beans to the barista who crafts your latte with artistic precision. We're proud to be a space where conversations flow as freely as our espresso.
